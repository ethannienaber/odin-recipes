Pesto Pasta

This top-rated pesto pasta recipe, which comes together in just 15 minutes, is the perfect quick and easy weeknight dinner.

Pesto pasta is a dish featuring pasta tossed with pesto, a flavorful sauce originating from Genoa, Italy. Pesto typically consists of basil, garlic, pine nuts, olive oil, and Parmesan cheese (or other hard Italian cheeses). The sauce is traditionally made by crushing these ingredients together, often using a mortar and pestle.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package pasta
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ cup chopped onion
  • 2 ½ tablespoons pesto or more to taste
  • salt to taste
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
